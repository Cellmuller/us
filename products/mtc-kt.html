The main cause of premature ejaculation is the inability to control ejaculation. Let's gradually increase the control of ejaculation by getting used to stimulation with the 5 incremental-stimuli masturbation cups.

Not just getting used to stimulation, but learning to control ejaculation through the movements of the pelvic floor muscles.

In addition to "getting used to the stimulus," this training has another purpose.
It is to learn how to control ejaculation by "manipulating the pelvic floor muscles". The pelvic floor muscles are the muscles that contract to stop urine during urination and to tighten the anus.
If the pelvic floor muscles are tense (strained), it becomes difficult to hold back ejaculation, so you will learn how to consciously relax the pelvic floor muscles.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q.Can it be used repeatedly?

A.The MEN'S TRAINNG CUP is a disposable product for hygiene reasons. Please refrain from reusing it or sharing it with others.

Q.How often and for how long should I train?

A.We recommend training four times a week for a total of seven weeks (28 sessions). If four times a week is difficult, you can reduce the frequency, but try to complete a total of 28 training sessions following the training guide.

Q.How should I proceed with the training?

A.Please follow the instructions in the training guide and complete the 4-step training (28 sessions in total).

Q. What is the ideal insertion time?

A.According to a survey of 449 cis women, the average ideal penetration time was 9.7 minutes (TENGA Healthcare survey, 2017). However, it is more important to consider whether you and your partner feel stressed about the penetration time. If you haven't discussed the penetration time with your partner, we recommend having a conversation about it.

Q.Can I still train even if I don't have a strong erection?

A.Training is possible as long as insertion is possible. As the level of the training cup increases, the material becomes harder, but if insertion is difficult, please use a cup with lower stimulation (softer material for easier insertion).

Q.If I stop using the training cup after training, will I return to my original state?

A.If you refrain from practicing the pelvic floor muscle control for a while, your body may forget the technique. We recommend having sexual intercourse regularly (once a week is recommended) or using the Level 5 CUP to ensure that you don't forget the control technique.

Q.There is a small hole near the insertion opening, is this a defect?

A.This hole (cut) is intentionally made during manufacturing to release air from inside the CUP during insertion. Please use it without any concerns.

Q.It is difficult to insert and I feel pain during insertion...

A. If insertion is difficult, please check the following points.
・Make sure you have removed the seal at the top of the product. If you leave the seal on, air cannot escape, making insertion difficult.
・If you experience pain during insertion, applying a little bit of lubricant to yourself can make insertion smoother. If you still experience pain, it may be because the size is not suitable, so please try a CUP with lower stimulation.
・If you are unable to insert because your penis is bent during insertion, there may not be enough firmness. Please try a CUP with a lower stimulation level (the gel will be softer and insertion will be easier).

Q.The lubricant dried out during training.

A.When the lubricant dries out, the sensation changes and it can interfere with training, so be careful. If you feel it is drying out, first try adding the lubricant remained on the lid of the CUP. If that is still not enough, please add lubricant to the product or your penis.

Q.When I was using it, a sponge came out from the inside, what is this?

A.The sponge is included to prevent the lubricant from drying out. Even if it comes out during use, there is no problem, so please continue to use it as it is.

Q.Even with Lv.1, the stimulation is too strong and it doesn't feel like training.

A.We recommend using the CUPs wearing a condom. By using a condom, you can weaken the stimulation.

Q.Even without doing STEP1 four times, I have become able to firmly be aware of the movement of the pelvic floor muscles. Can I proceed to STEP2?

A.Yes. If you become able to be aware of the movement of the pelvic floor muscles, it is fine to proceed to STEP2 without completing the training four times. However, from STEP2 onwards, please make sure to complete the required number of sessions before moving on to the next STEP.

Q.Is it okay to proceed to the next STEP even if I cannot comfortably do each STEP?

A.Yes. Once you have reached the set number of sessions, you can move on to the next STEP. Even if it is difficult to endure during training, if the control of the pelvic floor muscles has improved, there is a possibility that the penetration time during actual sexual intercourse may be extended as well.

Q.I can't hold ejaculation at all in STEP 2, 3, and 4.

A.Even if you can't hold it, there is a possibility that the pelvic floor muscle is being controlled, so please continue with the training. If you have no idea how to move the pelvic floor muscle, please refer back to STEP 0 and review how to move the pelvic floor muscle.

Q. I don't understand how to do the Hip Thrust Training in STEP 4.

A.We recommend fixing the CUP to a cushion or something similar. Please try training using the following method as a reference.

Q.I have done the training up to STEP 4, but I want to increase the insertion time even more.

A.We recommend continuing with the Hip Thtrust Training in STEP 4. Be more conscious of relaxing the pelvic floor muscles and engage in the training. Additionally, using the TENGA CUP WARMER can make it closer to actual sexual intercourse, so we recommend using it.

Q.I have completed all the training, but I still have a hard time enduring during actual sexual intercourse.

A.Even during actual sexual intercourse, let's be conscious of relaxing the pelvic floor muscles, just like during training. Also, the temperature difference between the training cup and the vagina may make it difficult to endure. In that case, try warming the training cup with the TENGA CUP WARMER before training.

Q.How should I dispose of the cup after use?

A. After use, please dispose it as plastic waste according to the rules of your local municipality.
