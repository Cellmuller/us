Frequently Asked Questions

Q. I don't know if I am late to ejaculate in the first place.

A. First, try a self-check. Also, if you can ejaculate within 15 minutes using the Lv.5 of MEN'S TRAINNG CUP Acceleration Training, it is considered normal, so we recommend trying it once.

Q.I can ejaculate during regular sexual intercourse, but I can't ejaculate during intercourse when trying to conceive.

A.The pressure during ovulation period for couples trying to conceive may be the cause. Erection and ejaculation are closely related to the autonomic nervous system, so even without clear awareness, a little pressure can disrupt the autonomic nervous system and make it difficult to have an erection or ejaculate. In this case, we recommend using at home insemination kit instead of the training cup.

Q.Can it be used repeatedly?

A.The MEN'S TRAINNG CUP are disposable products for hygienic reasons. Please refrain from reusing them or sharing them with others.

Q.How should I proceed with the training?

A.Please follow the instructions in the training guide.

Q.How often and for how long should I train?

A.We recommend training once or twice a week. According to medical records, training once a week takes an average of 14 weeks until graduation. However, the training period can vary from 1 week to 4 months depending on your condition at the start of the training.

Q.How long should the insertion time be?

A.When 449 women were surveyed about their "ideal insertion time," the average ideal insertion time was 9.7 minutes (TENGA Healthcare survey, 2017). However, it is not the length of insertion time that matters, but whether you and your partner feel stressed about the insertion time. If you haven't talked to your partner about the insertion time, we recommend discussing it once.

Q.My size is larger than average, can I still use it?

A.The diameter of the cup's insertion part is about 6 cm. Some people may have difficulty inserting it or may not be able to feel stimulation properly if their thickest part is about 4.5 cm. Please check your own size, and if it exceeds a diameter of 4.5 cm, it may be difficult to use. We apologize, but we currently do not offer different size options.

Q.Can I still train even if I don't have a good erection?

A.If you are unable to achieve an erection during actual sexual intercourse or have difficulty maintaining an erection for a certain period of time, you may have erectile dysfunction (ED). In cases of delayed ejaculation, difficulty ejaculating inside the vagina, and ED, it is necessary to address ED first. If you suspect ED, we recommend consulting a specialized medical institution, referring to self-checks. However, if you have a weak erection and find it difficult to insert, please try using a CUP with lower stimulation (the gel becomes softer and easier to insert).

Q.If I stop using the training cup after training, will I return to my original state?

A.After completing the training, we recommend regularly (recommended once a week) using CUP Lv.5 or TIMING TRAINER -ACCELERATION- to maintain your condition. Also, improper masturbation may cause you to return to your original state, so please be careful not to engage in improper masturbation. Please use a disposable CUP Lv.5 or a washable and reusable TIMING TRAINER -ACCELERATION- for maintenance training.

Q.There was a small hole near the insertion opening, is this a defect?

A.This hole (cut) is made during manufacturing to release air from the cup during insertion. Please use it without any concerns.

Q.It is difficult to insert and I feel pain during insertion...

A. If insertion is difficult, please check the following points.
・Make sure you have removed the seal at the top of the product. If you leave the seal on, air cannot escape, making insertion difficult.
・If you experience pain during insertion, applying a little bit of lubricant to yourself can make insertion smoother. If you still experience pain, it may be because the size is not suitable, so please try a CUP with lower stimulation.
・If you are unable to insert because your penis is bent during insertion, there may not be enough firmness. Please try a CUP with a lower stimulation level (the gel will be softer and insertion will be easier).

Q.The lube dried out during training.

A.When the lubricant dries out, the sensation changes and it may affect your training, so please be careful. If you feel it is drying out, try adding the remaining lubricant from the lid of the CUP. If that is still not enough, please add lubricant in the CUP.

Q.When I was using it, a sponge came out from inside. What is this for?

A.The sponge is included to prevent the lubricant from drying out. Even if it comes out during use, there is no problem, so please continue to use it without the sponge.

Q.Is there anything to be careful about jerk-off material when training?

A.It is recommended to avoid using stimuli that are too strong and unrealistic, as it may not provide sufficient visual stimulation during actual sexual intercourse. It is best to imagine the act with your partner.

Q.How quickly do I need to ejaculate within each level to progress to the next level?

A.The goal for each level is to ejaculate within 15 minutes, but even if you cannot ejaculate, you can still move on to the next level from Lv.2 to Lv.4. Ultimately, the goal is to be able to ejaculate within 15 minutes with Lv.5. If you have a partner, you can confirm how long it is ideal for them to ejaculate and use that as a goal.

Q.I could not ejaculate even at Lv.1.

A.Lv.1 may be too stimulating, and some people may find it difficult to ejaculate. In that case, please try using Lv.2 once. If you still cannot ejaculate, it is recommended to consult a specialist.

Q. I can ejaculate at Lv.5, but I cannot ejaculate during actual sexual intercourse.

A.We recommend that you fix Lv.5 and train by shaking your hips as in actual sexual intercourse. Please refer to here. If you wish to train with weaker stimulation than Lv.5, we recommend using Men's Training Cup Endurance Training Lv.1 or TIMING TRAINER -ACCELERATION-.

Q.How should I dispose of the cup after use?

A.After use, please dispose of it as plastic waste according to the rules of your local municipality.
